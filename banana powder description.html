About Banana Powder

Banana powder is a fine, off-white to light yellow powder made from processed bananas. It's widely used in the food industry for making baby foods, milkshakes, cakes, and biscuits.

  • Rich in potassium and dietary fiber
  • Gluten-free and 100% natural
  • Enhances flavor and nutrition of food products
  • Used in pharmaceutical and cosmetic products
